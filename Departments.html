Engineering Departments

Biomedical, Biological and Chemical Engineering

Research is broad, where they work closely with medicine, veterinary medicine, nutrition, and natural resources. Undergraduate students can get involved starting freshman year.

Civil & Environmental Engineering

Focus areas of research includes environmental/water resources, geotechnical, structural, and transportation. Undergraduate students can get involved starting freshman year.

Electrical Engineering and Computer Science

Some of the research areas include Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, Physical and Power Electronics, and Control and Robotics. Undergraduate students can get involved starting freshman year.

Industrial and Manufacturing Systems Engineering

Focus areas of research includes Human-Computer Interaction, Quality and Reliability Engineering, Data Analytics for Decision Making, and Healthcare Systems. Undergraduate students can get involved starting freshman year.

Information Technology

Departmental research includes Autonomous Systems Lab, Research Environments for Extended Reality Lab, and Virtual Reality Lab. It is strognly encouraged to get involved as early as freshman year.

Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering

Some of the focus areas include Fluid Power Systems, Aerospace Navigation, Thermal Management, and Metamaterials. Undergraduate students can get involved starting freshman year.
